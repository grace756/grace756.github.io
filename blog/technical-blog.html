The difference between Margin, Border and Padding

  • Content-The content box is where images and text are i.e the content of the page your'e working on
  • Padding- Padding is an invisable predetermined space around the content
  • Border-The border sits outside the padding forming the next layer of the 'box'
  • Margin- Following the border there is a margin , the margin fills the rermainder of the space completing the 'box' and separates differnt elements
